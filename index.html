About this app


I'm a huge fan of manga. Like very huge. Beside Dota 2, what's better than lying down reading manga on your phone?

So on one useless day I was re-reading One Piece and hell it's fucking hard to read on phone. The mobile site was pretty awful and lacked basic controls. I decided to find an app on the Play Store that could somehow make my manga experience a little bit brighter. Guess what, almost all the native apps there were outdated (last update first half of 2014). Some were decent but not available in my country(?) or being filled with ads (I'm not against placing ads on the app, but not ads that cover the content. I was like "I'm here to read manga not to mess with your ads you greedyfuck").

At that time I was a noob at Android development (I still am, only to a lesser extent now). Had some apps but pretty much garbage. Jump Manga is my first fully-featured app that targets a larger audience, with the help of some of my friends. Being a manga addict myself, it made everything easier in the sense that I could quickly form an idea on how the app would look like and what the user truly needed. I guess that's the beauty of app development, when you are not limited by a long list of dos and don'ts from your employer you tend to have more fun working - and working more efficient, too.

However this is no bed of roses. Google fucking took down the app 2 times in a row and responded me with auto reply. When my Android career was just one more strike from going down, I had to change the app name (yes it sounds so awkward now) and performed a bunch of long ass process to avoid copyright issues. And finally I managed to get it on Play Store through my elder friend account. Fuck Google and their robots.

TL;DR: Jump Manga was developed not only to serve my needs of everyday manga, but also act as a product for my future CV. My GPA is fucking low I get a heart attack everytime I think about it. So please help me getting away from unemployment by trying this app, I'm sure that you won't be disappointed üê£
